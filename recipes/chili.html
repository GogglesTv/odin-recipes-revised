Homemade Chili

Description

Nothing is better in the fall than a warm and hearty chili with beans! This chili is also special because it's made with a slow cooker. This is a very simple and easy recipe that you're able to let sit-and-cook all on its own. Bookmark this page because you gone be back... trust.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 2 16oz cans kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 28oz can diced tomatoes(I personally use fire-roasted)
  • 1 15oz can tomato purée
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 4oz can chopped green chile peppers
  • 2 tbsp mild chili powder
  • 2 tsp salt(I personally use Redmond Ancient Fine Sea Sal)
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Add ground beef; cook and stir until browned, 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Transfer beef mixture to a 6-quart slow cooker. Stir in kidney beans, diced tomatoes, tomato purée, water, green chile peppers, chili powder, salt, cumin, and black pepper.
  3. Cook on low until flavors combine, 4 to 6 hours.
